Terms of Use:

Below are the Terms of Use for the 2014 Anime and Manga Stack Exchange Gift Exchange.

We have kept these short and to the point. Please make sure to read everything. The instructions to continue with your form application are inside.

Disambiguation:

Participant
Any of the eligible users of the site that participates in the 2014 Christmas Gift Exchange. Every participant is both a gifter and a giftee (definitions below).
Gifter
The person sending the gift.
Giftee
The person receiving the gift.
SE
Stack Exchange
  1. The participants agree to fulfill all of the below requirements. The consequence of not doing so will be the exclusion from similar future community events. (alternatively reparations can be made by making a donation on behalf of the giftee or Anime.SE to a registered/recognized charity of your choice)
  2. The gifter will not send send/distribute anything harmful, disturbing, or illegal (physically or mentally) to the giftee and their respective country, providence, or locale.
  3. The participant acknowledges that this event is formed by the community and not, in any way, endorsed by or associated with Stack Exchange or its management and operators.
  4. The participants agree that neither Stack Exchange nor the site is responsible for damage or loss of the parcel (including confiscation), any additional taxes or fees levied in the process of delivery.
  5. If the gifter has any circumstances where they feel they cannot participate, due to personal, economical, or other reasons (excluding laziness and procrastination), they will message a moderator about this and their reasons before the shipping deadline. (might require a charitable donation be made on behalf of your giftee or Anime.SE)
  6. The deadlines are as follows: Opt-in phase - Up to the end of October; Gift selection phase - Up to mid November; Shipping phase - Up to the end of December, preferably arriving before the end of the year.
  7. After the end of the Opt-in phase the participants will be attributed (in the following two days) a giftee, which they (the gifter) will contact directly in order to get their (the giftee's) contact and shipping information. Preferred formatting includes your Anime.SE username as c/o (example: Asami Sanada 411 Gamers Rd Apt. 723, c/o Dejiko Winter Garden, DG Broccoli
    8. Country).
  8. By entering the words: "I've read all of these terms" in the opt-in form, the gifter does not have to pick something from a giftee's wishlist, the only (soft) requirement being that the gifts be somewhat anime or manga related. The suggested value for the gifts is $20 USD or the economic equivalent. Gifts can be digital if the gifter does not have the means of sending a physical parcel at a reasonable rate, researching beforehand to ensure that the giftee will not have trouble getting access to it in their respective country.
  9. Once the gift is sent, the gifter will inform the giftee by email, along with a tracking number (when available). If, for some reason, the gifter feels they will not be able to make the deadline for the shipping phase, they will contact the giftee directly informing them of the occurrence.
  10. The giftee will inform the gifter once the gift has arrived. Afterwards, all the participants will post pictures(s) of their gift on a meta post (which will later be created specifically for this purpose). The participants may choose to share an Imgur album if they prefer, along with their reactions to the gifts.
  11. If the giftee has received no gift or been informed that their gift is on the way by the shipping deadline, they will contact any of the moderators, so that they can take care of solving their issue as soon as possible.
  12. If the participants have any issues during the process, they will contact the moderators.
